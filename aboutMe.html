How to take care of a Dog

To take care of a dog, follow these vet-recommended tips:
  • Feeding: Provide proper nutrition and feeding
  • Exercise: Ensure regular exercise between 30 minutes and two hours daily.
  • Grooming: Keep your dog clean and reduce shedding with frequent brushing.
  • Safe Environment: Provide a safe living space.
  • Regular Veterinary Checkups: Bring your dog to the vet regularly.
  • Training and Socialization: Interact with your dog and socialize them.
  • Identification and Licensing: Ensure proper identification.
  • Safe Handling: Handle your dog safely
